About

Hi! I'm a self taught programmer - and specialise in games and game-adjacent things. My primary language is C++ but Rust is my preference for all my personal projects
I've also dabbled with C#, D, Python, JS, Go, Clojure, and good ol' x86 Assembly.

I completed a Bachelor in Game Development at SAE Institute Brisbane in 2016. Spent much of that time learning Unity and getting a working knowledge of Blender down

I have several years experience with OpenGL, and am well acquainted with web tech like emscripten and webassembly. I also have some limited experience with Android NDK

  • Tools Programmer at Creative Assembly jun 2018 - now
  • Gameplay/Engine Programmer at Game Insight aug 2016 - nov 2017
  • Intern (QA/Programming) at Euclideon 2015
  • Trainee at Valhalla Bifrost 2012

Born in NZ, raised in Australia, currently in Horsham, UK
