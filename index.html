Who am I

I'm a self taught programmer and I specialise in games and game-adjacent things.

My primary language is C++ but Rust is my preference for all my personal projects.

I've also used C#, Python, and JS on the odd occasion, and can read x86 Assembly when I absolutely must

I have several years experience with OpenGL, and am well acquainted with web tech like emscripten and webassembly. I also have some limited experience with Android NDK

Born in NZ, raised in Australia, currently in Horsham, UK
